Services

Coloring $200+


Coloring (Going Darker)

This process involves going from light to dark, selecting the overall tone or tones you would like in the hair.

Balayage (Going Lighter)

involves going lighter and selecting the overall tone or tones you wish to have in your hair.

Color Correction $250+


This process involves a drastic transformation that can take many different specified formulas and also quite some time in the salon. It can be anything from removing artificial color from the hair to achieve a desired shade. OR using multiple coats of color in a specified permanent formula to eliminate "mistake tones" such as; orange, yellow, red and green." Followed by the removal process, you would pick a new tone, (darker, the same level, or lighter.)
*grey coverage is standard with all color services
*Olaplex is standard with ALL color services

Haircut $85+

This service includes a "custom made" precision haircut depending on your specific hair type. It can range from specified layers for baby fine hair to make it feel & appear thicker OR specialized texturizing and shaping for thick hair to make it appear less frizzy & easy to style.

Toner/Touch-Up $85-200+

Global Kertain $300+

After searching long and hard, I have finally found a keratin treatment that is safe, reminds me of the Brazilian Blowout, but does not hurt me or my clients." This is a formaldehyde FREE smoothing treatment that actually works. It is safe for fine hair that is damaged, all the way to thick, frizzy & curly hair that needs serious smoothing. This treatment does not edit the curl or wave pattern of the hair. This product allows the cuticle to turn the same direction, reducing "frizz" and adding shine by up to 90%. The product is processed in the shampoo bowl, then flat ironed into the clients hair, sealing the cuticle and permanently installing the treatment.
